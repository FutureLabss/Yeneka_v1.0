About us

Yeneka Nigeria

Small FMCG businesses and local retail stores have each others lifeline - inventory and distribution, respectively.

However, small business owners hardly find local stores that accept to buy & retail their products(e.g popcorn, peanuts, chinchin, liquid soaps, etc,.) for the first time.

Consequently, small business owners are starting out by partnering with familiar neighbourhood retailers to sell their products & pay afterwards

Such partnerships are often in small isolated units since it has to do with mutual agreements and trust.

Yeneka scales these partnerships through B2B Inventory barter.

How it works,

❶
You partner with a local retail store that can either;

- Pay you for the products you will get for it. Or,

- Agrees to credit, I.e, to pay you after selling the products.

❷

- Know what your retailer needs to restock [liquid soap, for example] & request it on our WhatsApp Business or status.

❸
[Let's say you make popcorn.]

- We match a local business that makes liquid soap & its retailer needs popcorn.

- We clear, the soap is delivered to your retailer & you give your popcorn to be taken to another store in exchangel,.

That way,

  • Products get placed around easily & in stores that need it.

[NB. Products are exchanged on market price & total price difference is settled in money.]

About us

Yeneka Nigeria

Small FMCG businesses and local retail stores have each others lifeline - inventory and distribution, respectively.

Typically, a small business which makes soap for example, would partner with one or two stores and place its product.

These partnerships are often in small isolated units since it has to do with mutual relationships, trust and credit.

However, the stores and the FMCG businesses need more than one of each other to grow.

Yeneka scales these partnerships through local inventory barter.

Put simply,

Step 1: My partner(store) has popcorn out of stock and yours soap, we tell Yeneka.

Step 2: Yeneka matches us, since you make popcorn and I make soap.

Step 3: We're cleared to meet and barter and we restock our partners(store).

That way,

  • My soap & your popcorn gets placed easily in a new store and,
  • Our partners(stores) get a wider product selection.

A win-win situation which still has the close relationship between a store owner and a FMCG business owner who know each other, intact.

About us

Yeneka Nigeria

Small FMCG businesses and local retail stores have each others lifeline - inventory and distribution, respectively.

Typically, a small business which makes soap for example, would partner with one or two stores and place its product.

These partnerships are often in small isolated units since it has to do with mutual relationships, trust and credit.

However, the stores and the FMCG businesses need more than one of each other to grow.

Yeneka scales these partnerships through inventory barter.

Put simply - it lets small FMCG businesses barter their products with each other to restock their partner-store.

That way,

  • Inventory gets distributed easily to different stores and,
  • Retail stores get a wider product selection.

A win-win situation which still has the close relationship between a store owner and a business owner who know each other, intact.

How Barter Works

You select a brand and request a barter. We confirm and clear for a meet-and-barter in your partner-store.

Parties can carry out inpection of products, set their favorite brands and report a fraud.

Inventory(products) is bartered(traded) on its value(price).

About us

Yeneka NG

We offer local & small-scale retailers aka, 'provision shop owners' an easy way to order your products & restock with WhatsApp.

Our vouchers lets us handle offline payments for shipping - so that you have a smoother delivery of your products.

With us, retailers access a wider selection of local brands at best prices, & product business owners make more sales within town.

How it works,

❶

Sign up
